SIZE GUIDE

How to Measure

Measure your body (not the garment) while wearing light clothing. Use a soft measuring tape and keep it parallel to the ground.

Oversized Fit

Our oversized tees are cut 3–4cm wider on the chest and 4–5cm longer than the standard unisex tee. If you prefer a more relaxed look, size up one. If you like it fitted, go true to size.

Tips

🧵 All Semboyan tees are 100% cotton and may shrink slightly after the first wash. We recommend cold washing and air drying to maintain the fit and print quality.

📏 If you're between sizes, size up — our tees are designed to wear with a relaxed drape.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed the next business day.

Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your parcel via Pos Malaysia or J&T Express.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 7 days of receiving your order, provided the item is unworn, unwashed, and in its original condition with tags intact.

To initiate a return, email us at hello@semboyanstreetwear.my with your order ID and reason for return.

Non-Returnable Items

Sale items, pre-order items, and items marked as final sale cannot be returned or exchanged.

Damaged or Wrong Items

If you received a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with a photo. We will send a replacement at no extra cost.

FAQ

When do new drops happen?
New drops happen every Friday at 8PM. Follow us on Instagram and TikTok to get notified first. Limited quantities — once it's gone, it's gone!
What does pre-order mean?
Pre-order means the item hasn't been printed yet. You pay now, we print your order and ship within 2–3 weeks. Pre-orders are limited and guarantee you get the design before it sells out.
What material are your tees made of?
All our tees are made from 100% organic cotton, 180–200gsm. Soft, breathable, and built to last. We don't do polyester blends — cotton only, like your nenek would approve of.
How do I wash my Semboyan tee?
Cold wash only (30°C max). Turn inside out before washing to protect the print. Do not tumble dry — air dry only. Do not iron directly on the print.
Can I cancel or change my order?
Orders can be cancelled or changed within 12 hours of purchase. After that, the order goes into processing. Email hello@semboyanstreetwear.my immediately if you need changes.
Do you ship internationally?
Not yet — we currently ship within Malaysia only. International shipping is coming soon. Follow us to stay updated!
Are your tees on Shopee or Lazada?
Yes! You can also find us on Shopee and Lazada. However, the best selection and exclusive drops are always on semboyanstreetwear.my first.
I have a question not listed here.
DM us on Instagram @semboyanstreetwear or email hello@semboyanstreetwear.my. We reply within 24 hours on business days.

ABOUT US

SEMBOYAN.STREETWEAR
Born in Malaysia · Built for the Culture

Semboyan Streetwear was born from a simple belief — that where you come from is something to be proud of, not ashamed of.

We are a Malaysian streetwear brand rooted in Tamil typography, local slang, and the kind of cultural pride that doesn't need to shout — it just wears it.

WHY SEMBOYAN?

Semboyan means motto or slogan in Malay. Every piece we make carries a message — a statement of identity for a generation that grew up between cultures and refuses to forget any of them.
